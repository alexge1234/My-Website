About

The person
behind the send.

I am a B2B marketer with about five years of experience, currently running multi-channel campaigns across email, web, social, and live events for national medtech, engineering, and manufacturing trade shows. Email and lifecycle work is the center of how I think. I like the discipline of it: a clear offer, a reason to open, and a number on the other side that tells you whether it worked.

My instinct is to work behind the scenes and make other people look good, whether that is a sales team walking into an event with the right collateral or a registrant who got exactly the email they needed at the right moment. I take a systematic, test-and-learn approach and would rather earn a result than guess at one.

I am certified across HubSpot and Google Analytics, and I keep building platform-portable expertise that travels with me, from enterprise tools like Eloqua to HubSpot and beyond.
